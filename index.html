What issues need to be considered for backup and restoration of data?

  • Important data is backup daily base
  • At least need three different version stored different locations
  • Fast and reliable hardware to support backup
  • Cost, backups cost money. You may have to buy hardware and software, pay for a maintenance agreement, and training for the staff members.
  • Backup location is important, so nowadays many companies have their backups to the cloud. However, companies should still consider potentially keeping a copy of their data in another location as well
  • Backup method can be chosen from different kinds of backups. Each backup method requires a different amount of storage, impacting costs, and a different amount of time, just companies have to keep in mind the length of the backup procedure and the length of the recovery.
  • Recovery flexibility is when creating backups, companies generally want to backup everything, but thatâ€™s not true for recovery. Recovery needs to be able to scale from restoring a single file to restoring an entire server.
  • Backup schedule should be automated and run on a schedule, not rely on someone remembering to execute them manually. Recovery point objective and recovery time objective come into play here.
  • Scalable can expect your data to grow and the backup needs to grow along with it. backup process should be able to handle expected volumes of new data.
  • Backup security needs to be accessible when needed, but they shouldnâ€™t be accessible by just anyone. Making sure backups are safe from tampering and protect the business.
